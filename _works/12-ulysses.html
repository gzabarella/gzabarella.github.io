Fucking Ulysses!
About a certain incapacity
of living in the present.
This wanting to go further ahead,
don’t you know that the earth is spheric?
You’ll end up on the other side of it,
and at some point
exactly at the same place where you started.
In the meantime, you’ll have lost
valuable things
because you were looking for something else
“further”! ---
