--- title: "Come to the places of pain" slug: cometotheplacesofpain carousel_image: /assets/images/cometotheplacesofpain/cttpop_1.jpg main_image: /assets/images/cometotheplacesofpain/cttpop_2.jpg year: 2020-2026 technique: > Soundtrack (stereo, 23’), forged receipts and parking tickets, car ride (20’) brief: > Passers-by in a Munich parking lot are invited to take a car ride on a Saturday evening during rush hour. The ride takes about 20 minutes and returns to the starting point each time. The car is crammed with piles of old receipts, train cards, parking tickets that look like discarded leftovers from past trips. During the drive, fragments of stories about border crossings, forgotten interiors, houses and ruins play from the car speakers, both through the narration of a male and a female voice and through the receipts, which function as a sort of takeaway librettos of the sound piece. What do we keep, when we leave a place? What sticks to us of the past? Does that place change and morph in the meantime, as some kind of autonomous self-healing body? The texts result from a research residency in Georgia, Abkhazia and Azerbaijan in autumn 2019.

First showing (as performance): La fange du macadam, group show, Munich 2020
Second showing (as radio play): Unset Time and Pace, PARA Sound Festival, Radio 80000, Munich 2021
Third showing (as performance, site-specific installation, 6-channel sound): Und die Blumen riechen trotzdem: Renate Gier-Francke und Giulia Zabarella, Museum Fürstenfeldbruck, 2026

Voices: Titus Wonsey, Giulia Zabarella
